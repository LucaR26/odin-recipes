Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients , which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Wheat-based dough
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Various other ingredients

Steps to prepare

Making the dough

  1. Place warm water in a large bowlof a heaby duty hand mixer. Sprinkle the yeast, and wait 5 minutes, until the yeast begins to foam.
  2. Add the flour, salt, sugar and olive oil and mix on medium speed for 7-10 minutes
  3. Let the dough rise, either in a fridge for 24 hours, for a slow rise, or in a warm place for an hour and a half, for a quick rise

Preparing the pizza

  1. Preheat the pizza stone at 475 degrees Fahrenheit for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Divide the dough into 2 balls
  3. Prepare your desired toppings
  4. Flatten the dough ball, and strech it out into a circle
  5. Spread tomato sauce and put on your toppings
  6. Bake the pizza in the oven for 10 to 15 minutes
