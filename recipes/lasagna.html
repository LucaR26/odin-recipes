Steps to prepare

  1. Brown the beef, combined with onion and garlic in a pan
  2. Pour 1/4 cup of white and stir until almost evaporated
  3. Combine all of the cheese sauce ingredients and 1 cup of mozzarella in a bowl
  4. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit and cook the lasagna noodle in a pot of salted boiling water
  5. Assemble, putting a layer of noodle, a layer of meat sauce and a layer of cheese
  6. Bake it for 45 minutes
